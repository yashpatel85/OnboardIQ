**Factorial24 Employee Handbook**

1. WORK HOURS:
Standard hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST, Monday through Friday. 
Flexible timing is allowed between 8:00 AM and 8:00 PM as long as core hours (11:00 AM - 4:00 PM) are covered.

2. LEAVE POLICY:
- Casual Leave (CL): 12 days per year.
- Sick Leave (SL): 10 days per year.
- Privilege Leave (PL): 15 days per year (accrued after probation).
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks.

3. REMOTE WORK:
Factorial24 operates on a hybrid model. Employees are required to be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
